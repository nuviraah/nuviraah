Our Story

Nuviraah was born out of a deep love for purity, care, and wellness. Inspired by timeless Ayurvedic wisdom and backed by modern research, we create gentle, safe, and effective products for moms and their precious little ones.

Our journey began with a simple idea — to offer chemical-free, cruelty-free products made with real ingredients that you can trust. From belly oils to baby creams, every product is crafted with intention and love.
